 About us


Let us tell you about our expertise


SEG3125 Animal Hospital has been providing veterinary care in Ottawa for 30 years. We are passionate about helping pets and educating owners. As a mid-sized practice catering to dogs and cats, we offer the utmost in personalized care from experienced vets. Your furry friend will feel at home in our caring, welcoming environment. We provide a modern facility which is kept clean and is staffed by happy professionals.

Service Description Price
General medicine We offer inpatient and outpatient medical treatment for eye, ear, skin, gastrointestinal,
respiratory, cardiac, urogenital, renal and hepatic diseases.		 $ 30
Vaccinations and
parasite prevention		 Billings Bridge Animal Hospital offers vaccination programs consistent with the American
Association of Animal Hospital (AAHA) vaccine guidelines. We carry a range of medications to
prevent intestinal worms, heartworm, and external parasites such as fleas, ticks and mange.		 $ 100
Surgical care We provide surgical services ranging from routine spays and neuters to more complex bladder,
ear, eye, gastrointestinal, orthopedic and tumour removal procedures. Our veterinarians are
experienced surgeons, and we can call upon the services of a mobile specialist if orthopaedic
work is needed. We also provide soft tissue surgery and tonometry procedure as well.		 $ 200-500
Dentistry In addition to our medical and surgical services, we are fully equipped with the latest in
dental equipment, including digital dental radiography. We offer routine cleanings (prophylaxis)
and dental extractions.		 $ 50
